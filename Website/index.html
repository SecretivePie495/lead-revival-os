The Challenge

Most Agencies Leave Significant Revenue in Their Existing Lead Data

Agencies running paid campaigns accumulate large volumes of leads that did not convert on the first contact. Rather than re-engaging those leads, most teams default to purchasing new ones.

Leads that have previously expressed interest in your client's offer are substantially less expensive to re-engage than acquiring net-new prospects. Lead Revival OS provides the infrastructure to do this systematically, across every client you manage.

  • 💸 Ad budget spent on leads that never moved past initial contact
  • 📂 Inactive leads sitting in CRMs with no follow-up structure
  • 🔁 No consistent process for re-engaging prospects at scale
  • 📊 Limited visibility across client accounts — each managed separately

How It Works

From Inactive Lead to Booked Call
in Three Steps

A structured workflow built for agencies managing multiple clients and high volumes of dormant paid leads.

STEP 01
📥
Import and Assign to Client

Connect your lead sources — Facebook, Google, Airtable, or CSV — and assign leads to the appropriate client workspace. Each account remains fully isolated with its own pipeline and data.

Airtable · Facebook · Google · CSV
STEP 02
⚡
Launch Re-Engagement Sequences

Apply pre-built or custom playbooks to initiate structured follow-up. Each lead moves through the pipeline from New → Contacted → Replied → Qualified as responses come in.

Kanban · Playbooks · Inbox
STEP 03
📅
Convert Replies into Bookings

When a lead responds, the Inbox surfaces the full conversation history for your team. Update the lead status, book the call, and track recovered pipeline value per client in Analytics.

Inbox · Analytics · Portal
Features

A Complete Toolkit for
Lead Re-Engagement Operations

◼
Multi-Client Workspace

Manage all client accounts from a single dashboard. Switch between clients in one click — every view, report, and pipeline is fully scoped to the selected account with no data overlap.

Client Selector · Account Isolation
▦
Kanban Pipeline Board

Visualize every lead's position across status columns: New, Contacted, Replied, Qualified, and Booked. Drag to update status, with changes syncing to your data source in real time.

Drag & Drop · Real-Time Sync
✉
Conversation Inbox

A centralized messaging interface scoped to each client. View the full conversation history for any lead, respond directly, and update their pipeline status without switching tools.

Client-Scoped · Full History
▷
Re-Engagement Playbooks

Build and deploy structured outreach sequences for dormant leads. Choose from pre-built templates or create custom ones — set timing, messaging, and conditions per campaign.

Sequences · Templates · Scheduling
📊
Analytics & Reporting

Track reply rates, booked calls, conversion rates, and recovered pipeline value. All charts and KPIs are scoped to the active client and persist across sessions via cookie.

KPIs · Charts · Trends

Client Portal

Provide clients with a secure, branded portal to view their pipeline and conversation history. Read-only access, separate from the operations dashboard, with Supabase-powered authentication.

Supabase Auth · Read-Only Access
